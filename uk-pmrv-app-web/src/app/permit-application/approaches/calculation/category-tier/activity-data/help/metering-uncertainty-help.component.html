Calculating overall metering uncertainty

All of the meters and methods relating to your measurement of the amount of fuel or material you are using within this source stream category must be accounted for.

E.g. Your fuel may come from a single tank and be routed to 5 different emission sources. If your meters for this fuel are measuring the amount of fuel used by each emission source, you must include each meter.

Overall uncertainty must be determined by squaring the uncertainty of each individual meter, adding the values together and then taking square root.
